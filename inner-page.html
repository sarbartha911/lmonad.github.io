FriendsTogether : thoughts to make things better and easy

I was founded on a virtual coffee table discussions. 4 college mates sat together and started discussing issues they faced during their Uni days. It was a video call, and quite casually they were discussing how it would be if all the friends come together and try to address the problems that people face, starting with a project to address the engineering students' issues. That was how I was born. Simple? The actual challenge is still to be unfolded.

I am an aspiring new age product development company, with a focus mainly on how to resolve issues of certain type and give a solution which is handy and easy. The product should be backed up by good AI, machine learning and analytics, and should show some progress in some form or the other. The target is to build robust solutions, which can be in the forms of software, websites, mobile applications, and to use all the latest technologies. The main aim would always be to help a customer to identify the root causes of their problems, and finally assist them.
