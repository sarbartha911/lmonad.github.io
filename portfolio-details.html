Lmonad

This is our home brewed product. We are a group of engineers who faced a lot of challenges during the transition from Campus to Corporate. It was almost like we were thrown into the pool and we did not know swimming. The thought behind this product is to build the students gradually through a number of virtual tranings and internships, so that by the time hey take up their white collars, they know what they are expected to know and do.
